# 1 - Planner Output

# TASK_DECOMPOSITION

- Analyze current revenue base and margin structure to determine if investment in subscription infrastructure (billing, fulfillment, customer support) can be absorbed without materially impacting profitability
- Identify top 2-5 performing SKUs by revenue, margin contribution, repeat purchase rate, and customer satisfaction scores to determine subscription anchor products
- Calculate baseline conversion rates for similar upsell/cross-sell initiatives in the last 12 months to establish realistic pilot conversion benchmarks
- Map weekly sales trends across the past 12-18 months to identify seasonal patterns and determine optimal launch timing for Q3 pilot
- Size the 5% pilot cohort by customer segment (high-value vs. at-risk vs. new) and model expected CLV uplift scenarios at 5%, 10%, and 15% conversion rates
- Define kill switch criteria (minimum conversion rate, churn threshold, support ticket volume, margin erosion) with measurable thresholds for go/no-go decision
- Build operational readiness checklist with Finance, Operations, and Customer Support teams covering billing system integration, priority shipping logistics, and staffing requirements
- Create measurement framework tracking CLV change, subscription retention rate, organic product sales impact, and customer satisfaction delta for pilot cohort

# MISSING_INFO

- Current blended margin percentage and fixed vs. variable cost structure to assess investment feasibility
- Existing repeat purchase rate and cohort retention curves to establish baseline for CLV comparison
- Cost breakdown for subscription program (platform fees, priority shipping incremental cost, discount burn rate, support overhead)
- Historical performance data for top 10-15 SKUs including revenue, units sold, margin, return rate, and repurchase frequency
- Customer segmentation data showing what percentage of base qualifies as "high-value" and their existing purchase behavior
- Competitive intelligence on similar subscription offerings in the category (pricing, features, reported adoption rates)
- Technical dependencies and timeline for billing system integration and kill switch implementation
- Support capacity analysis to handle potential 5% cohort inquiries without degrading service levels

# DECISION_CRITERIA

- **Revenue risk management** — Minimize cannibalization of existing full-price purchases while maximizing incremental CLV lift
- **Operational feasibility** — Ensure current infrastructure and team capacity can support subscription fulfillment without quality degradation
- **Customer trust and satisfaction** — Deliver clear value proposition that strengthens brand perception rather than creating perception of paywall
- **Financial return** — Achieve positive contribution margin on subscription revenue within pilot period to justify full rollout investment
- **Learning velocity** — Structure pilot to generate statistically significant insights within 8-12 weeks to inform decision timeline
- **Reversibility** — Maintain ability to gracefully exit or pivot without lasting customer relationship or brand damage
