- NovaCart is considering launching a Premium Subscription Tier for its top-performing product line
- The subscription would offer priority shipping, exclusive discounts, and early access to new products
- Target is to increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) by 15% within 6 months
- Current blended CLV is approximately $1,420
- Rollout plan includes a 5% customer cohort pilot with a kill switch

- Three options are under consideration:
  - (A) Launch pilot in Q3 with top 2 SKUs
  - (B) Delay until full product catalog analysis is complete
  - (C) Launch a limited free-tier first to gauge interest

# RISK_TRIGGERS

## Financial Risks
- **Margin erosion** — Subscription discounts and priority shipping costs exceed incremental revenue, creating negative unit economics
- **Revenue cannibalization** — Existing high-value customers downgrade total spend or shift purchases exclusively to discounted subscription items
- **Cost overruns** — Implementation costs for billing systems, logistics, or support exceed budget without corresponding revenue to offset

## Operational Risks
- **Infrastructure failure** — Billing system integration incomplete or unstable, preventing reliable subscription management
- **Fulfillment breakdown** — Operations cannot consistently deliver priority shipping commitments, damaging customer trust
- **Support capacity breach** — Customer service team overwhelmed by subscription inquiries, degrading response times and satisfaction scores across all customers

## Customer & Brand Risks
- **Value perception failure** — Customers perceive subscription as unnecessary paywall rather than value-add, creating negative brand sentiment
- **Pilot cohort backlash** — Selected 5% cohort experiences technical issues or poor execution, creating vocal detractors
- **Non-subscriber alienation** — Customers excluded from pilot or who decline subscription feel penalized, reducing overall satisfaction

## Strategic Risks
- **Premature launch** — Insufficient data on top SKUs leads to poor product selection, undermining value proposition
- **Delayed decision** — Two-week timeline expires without key financial or operational data, forcing decision without adequate information
- **Kill switch unavailable** — Unable to implement graceful exit mechanism, leaving company committed to failed pilot with no recovery path
- **Stakeholder misalignment** — VP Product, Finance Lead, or Operations cannot commit necessary resources within timeline

## Measurement Risks
- **Insufficient sample size** — 5% cohort too small to generate statistically significant results within 6-month timeframe
- **Tracking gaps** — Inability to isolate subscription impact on CLV from other initiatives or market factors
- **Conflicting metrics** — Subscription retention high but organic product sales decline, creating ambiguous success signal
